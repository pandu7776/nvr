✦ About Me

Storyteller by Heart,
Developer by Trade

I'm NVR — a developer and author living in Paris, France, where the cobblestones and café lights have a way of finding their way into everything I build and everything I write.

By day, I architect digital worlds at VAS Labs, where I founded a team obsessed with building things that actually feel like something. Whether that's a Flutter app where the interface breathes like a story, or a DBMS schema so clean it disappears into the experience — my work is built on one conviction: function without feeling is just code.

By night — and in the quiet hours — I write. My stories live at the intersection of identity, longing, and place. From the neon-lit streets of Paris to questions of where we come from and who we become, I write to capture the moments that stay with you long after the page turns.

Part of the For Real team at VAS Labs. Part of every city I've passed through. I don't just develop — I compose.
